About

I don't just post content. I build platforms.

"It started with 100 strangers and a camera. It's leading me to the brand room."

In my second year of college, I took a vox pop project — 100 people, one day. That project turned into an internship. The internship led to news anchoring and reporting. And one thing kept leading to the next.

I moved into corporate content and never looked back. At ffreedom App (now BossWallah), I joined as Creative Director. At BankSathi, I built across anchor, writer and social media manager roles. At Apna, I came in as a Content Creator and within two years was promoted to Assistant Manager — Social Media.

At Apna, I didn't just manage a page — I built @jobssamachar from scratch to 82K followers organically in under a year, scripted and appeared in 500+ videos, ran email marketing, WhatsApp campaigns, directed ad films, ghostwrote for the CEO, and led a team of 10.

Now I'm ready for the next step: owning a brand fully. The platform-building experience, the strategic thinking, the end-to-end execution — it all points to Brand Management.
